In the actual Driver Knowledge Test (DKT), the computer will randomly select 45 questions for you from a question bank of a couple of hundred. There are three sections:
  1. General Driver Knowledge - 15 questions
  2. Road Safety - 20 questions
  3. Road Signs - 10 questions
You must get at least 12/15 questions correct (not more than 3 wrong) in the General Driver Knowledge section and you must get at least 29/30 questions correct (not more than 1 wrong) in the combined Road Safety and Road Signs sections.
