Driver Knowledge Test (DKT)

Demonstration Test
What is the main aim of the test? To test your knowledge of road rules and road safety in order to obtain a driver’s licence.
  • To make you familiar with the format of the computer based DKT.
  • To see the types of questions and topic areas you will attempt in the DKT.
How many questions are in the test? 45 20
What is the structure of the test? There are three sections:
  • General Driver Knowledge – 15 questions
  • Road Safety – 20 questions
  • Road Signs – 10 questions
 There are three sections:
  • General Driver Knowledge – 6 questions
  • Road Safety – 8 questions
  • Road Signs – 6 questions
Which questions will I be asked to answer? The computer will randomly select 45 questions for you from a question bank of several hundred.  You will always be asked the same 20 questions. The questions within each section will be displayed in random order.

These questions may NOT be in the actual DKT.

How do I pass the test?
  • You must get at least 12/15 questions right (not more than 3 wrong) in the General Driver Knowledge section and,
  • you must get at least 29/30 questions right (not more than 1 wrong) in the combined Road Safety and Road Signs sections.
 This is a demonstration test only. There are no PASS/FAIL criteria.When you complete the test you will receive a score for each section of the test.
Can the test be automatically terminated? Yes.When you select the 4th incorrect answer in the first section of the test (General Driver Knowledge), or the 2nd incorrect answer in the remaining two sections (Road Safety and Traffic Signs), the test is automatically terminated. You failed the test. No.You can always complete the test, however you may exit the Demonstration Test at any time by selecting the EXIT button.
Is there a time limit to complete the test? No No
How do I select the answer option of my choice? You touch the screen of the DKT computer on the selected option. You use a mouse to point and click on your selected option.
In what order do the three answer options appear? The options appear in random order. The options appear in random order.
How are the results of the test accessed? When you complete the test or the test is terminated, you will be asked to call a registry supervisor who will access the results screen. When you complete the last question, the results screen will automatically appear.
How can I best prepare to pass the DKT? Study carefully the appropriate road rules handbook/s :
  • Road Users’ Handbook
  • Motorcycle Riders’ Handbook
  • Heavy Vehicle Drivers’ Handbook
These are available free of charge from motor registries or the RTA internet site.

Practise the Demonstration Test to become familiar with the format of the DKT.

 The Demonstration Test will give you a good idea of what you can expect when you attempt the actual DKT. Correctly answering the 20 questions however is NOT enough. You need to study the appropriate road rules handbook/s to prepare for and pass the DKT.
Are there other types of knowledge tests for different licence classes? The DKT includes specific knowledge tests for:
  • Car drivers
  • Motorcycle riders
  • Light Rigid vehicle drivers
  • Medium Rigid vehicle drivers
  • Heavy Rigid vehicle drivers
  • Heavy Combination vehicle drivers
  • Multi Combination vehicle drivers
 The Demonstration Test contains car questions only. The format of the car test is the same as for all other types of knowledge tests.
What languages is the test available in? English, Arabic, Chinese, Croatian, Greek, Korean, Serbian, Spanish, Turkish, Vietnamese English only.
