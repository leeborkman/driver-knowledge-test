  • Run the Demonstration Test

1) In the test, when you click on the answer you think is correct, the line you have selected will turn grey. This is shown below. If you change your mind after you click on the answer, you can click on any of the other two answer options.

When you are sure about your answer, click the OK button at the bottom right hand corner of the screen.

2) If your answer is correct, a green arrow appears to the left of the answer you have selected and you will automatically go to the next question.

3a) If your answer is wrong, a red X appears to the left of the answer you have selected and a blue arrow appears next to the correct answer.

Click on the Next question button at the bottom right hand side of the screen to continue.

3b) If your answer is wrong and it is either the first or the last question, a red X appears to the left of the answer you have selected. A box appears at the bottom of the screen saying That was not the right answer, please try again.

To try again, click on the question you think is correct and then click on OK.

4) At each question (except the first and the last) you can do one of the following:

  • Answer the question
  • Skip the question by selecting the Skip question button, or
  • Exit the test by selecting the EXIT button.

6) When you finish the test, a Results Screen appears, showing your results for each test section. It also tells you what score you require to pass the actual DKT.

Close the results window to leave the Demonstration Test.
